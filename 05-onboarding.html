FAQ
? What is a BX Reference?
It's your unique deposit identifier. Loop uses it to automatically match incoming EFTs to your account and trigger minting. Think of it as your account number for minting.
? Can I have multiple wallets?
Yes. Additional wallet whitelist requests go through your Loop ops contact. At MVP, one wallet is active per account.
? What if I forget my BX Reference?
It's always visible in Settings → BX Reference and in the topbar on every page.
Need help?
Your Loop relationship manager is available Mon–Fri, 08:00–17:00 SAST. For urgent issues outside hours, contact ops@loop.finance.
